About Me

Hi, I'm Xander Rancap — a passionate software developer currently pursuing Software Development at the Southern Alberta Institute of Technology, where I maintain a 3.7 GPA. My journey combines technical skills, hands-on experience, and strong interpersonal abilities shaped through both academics and years of working in customer-focused environments.

In February 2024, I interned at Eduspec Holdings Berhad, a robotics-focused company based in Metro Manila. There, I worked with educational robotics platforms such as Arduino, SAM Labs, and VEX Robotics. My responsibilities included collaborating on and leading circuit design and programming tasks, which strengthened my ability to bridge hardware and software concepts. This experience allowed me to apply classroom knowledge to real-world challenges while refining leadership, teamwork, and problem-solving skills.

Beyond this, I’ve led several group projects applying object-oriented programming principles. For my OOP final project, I guided my team in building a cross-platform application using .NET MAUI Blazor Hybrid — blending web and native mobile technologies to deliver a seamless user experience. These projects not only sharpened my technical abilities but also taught me how to manage teams, delegate effectively, and design scalable, maintainable solutions.

My academic journey also included research initiatives during senior high school. One studied the effectiveness of Verdura Glow, a natural soap infused with Gliricidia sepium (Madre de Cacao) and Annona muricata (Guyabano) leaves, on itch relief and burn mark fading. Another explored the correlation between sleep deprivation and academic performance among Grade 12 Dentistry students at Centro Escolar Integrated School–Manila. While different from my current path, these projects built my skills in research, collaboration, and analysis — skills that continue to benefit me in software development.

My technical toolkit includes C#, Python, JavaScript, SQL, HTML, and CSS, along with UI/UX design skills in Figma. I’m also expanding my knowledge by learning Java, frameworks such as Node.js, React, and Tailwind, and diving into mobile development. These tools and technologies allow me to create clean, user-friendly, and efficient software that bridges functionality with design.

At my core, I’m passionate about using technology to solve real problems and create meaningful user experiences. Whether it’s through building robust back-end systems, designing intuitive front-end interfaces, or experimenting with emerging frameworks, I’m always eager to learn, grow, and collaborate with others in the tech space.
